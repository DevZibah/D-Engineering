    about darex engineering

    Darex Engineering Limited, an emerging Electro-Mechanical services company in Nigeria, which specializes in building services and infrastructures solution business, is engaged in the design, development, supply, installation, servicing and support of home and industrial infrastructures, including but not limited to Air-Conditioning systems, Electrical infrastructures (Wiring & Lightings), Telecommunication infrastructures (Cabling & Systems), Building Management Systems (BMS), Home Automation, CCTV systems, Fire Detection, Suppression and Alarm Systems, Electronic Article Surveillance Systems, Vehicle Tracking Systems, Home and Personal Security and Integrated systems. An infrastructure solutions provider, dedicated to the provision of total home and industrial infrastructure solutions that add value, luxury, comfort and security to lives.
