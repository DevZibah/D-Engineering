Fire alarm systems

FIRE... it rages and destroys all in its path, leaving its victims a little time to respond and extinguish it. However, statistics show that the greater percentage of raging fires can be effectively arrested in the early stages in which they shoulder. This fact forms the basis of our Fire Detection and Alarm Systems.

Intruder Alarm systems

At those times when your premises are empty or vulnerable to unauthorized entry or break-ins, it helps to have an effective intruder alarm system in place that watches the way you would... especially when you are not there!

Electronic Access control

The monitoring of access into and within any premises is the foremost security challenge faced by those in charge of security at such locations, basically due to the human nature’s “familiarity factor” which will normally tend to grant access into secure areas to familiar faces whether or not they are duly authorized at each time.

A reliable solution is the installation of an electronic access control system that will verify that each person is authorized for entry every single time... BEFORE access is granted!
